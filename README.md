## Overview

**KnightGate** is a 2D action-based game built around a growth and progression mechanic. The player takes control of a knight whose kingdom has fallen into danger after a sinister wizard opens a gateway to a corrupted realm. As hostile creatures spread across the land, the knight must survive increasingly difficult battles, gain power from defeated enemies, and push forward until the final confrontation with the wizard.

This README gives a compact summary of the game concept, gameplay systems, characters, controls, team contributions, and development resources.

---

## Story Premise

A powerful wizard has taken over a royal castle and torn open a portal to a dark dimension. From this breach, dangerous creatures flood into the kingdom, leaving destruction behind them. Corrupted soldiers, goblins, skeletons, and summoned beasts now serve under the wizard’s command.

To make matters worse, the wizard strengthens his army with magical potions that enhance their size, speed, and power. As these enemies become more threatening, the kingdom’s last hope is a brave knight determined to stop the invasion.

During his journey, the knight defeats enemies and absorbs the magical energy they leave behind. This allows him to grow stronger over time and prepare for the greater challenges ahead. His ultimate goal is to defeat the wizard, seal the portal, and reclaim peace for the kingdom.

---

## Gameplay Concept

The central idea behind **KnightGate** is progression through **scaling**. As the player advances, the knight becomes more powerful by overcoming enemies and absorbing magical effects linked to them.

### Main Gameplay Features
- **Growth-based progression:** Defeated enemies help the knight become stronger through a size-increase mechanic.
- **Level-to-level escalation:** Each stage introduces stronger enemies that require more strength to overcome.
- **Boss influence:** The final wizard boss can support his allies by summoning units and empowering them during combat.
- **Increasing challenge:** Enemy power, size, and pressure rise as the player progresses.
- **Basic combat system:** The knight can move, attack, and survive encounters using close-range sword combat.

## Player Actions

The player is able to perform the following in-game actions:

- Move across each level
- Jump over hazards and reach platforms
- Strike enemies using the sword
- Block incoming attacks
- Interact with potion-based scaling effects
- Pause and resume the game
- Navigate menus such as:
  - Start Game
  - Controls
  - Quit
  - Return to Menu

## Characters

### Protagonist

The Knight:
The knight is the central playable character and the protagonist of the game. His mission is to save the kingdom by battling the enemies unleashed by the wizard and ultimately destroying the source of the corruption.

### Enemies

#### Level 1 Enemies

Orc Shaman:
A staff-carrying enemy identified by white hair and a tribal appearance. This character is best suited to a support or ranged spellcasting role, bringing additional variety to combat through indirect magical attacks.

Orc Warrior:
A close-range enemy armed with a battle axe and defined by a strong, intimidating build. This unit serves as a frontline fighter and represents the primary melee threat among the Level 1 enemies.

Orc Berserker:
An aggressive melee enemy recognized by dual cleavers and a savage visual design. This unit is suited to intense close-quarters combat and adds a more dangerous and chaotic presence to the Level 1 enemy group.

#### Level 2 Enemies

Light Bandit:
A lightly armored humanoid enemy with a sword-based fighting style and a fast, agile appearance. This character is designed to function as a quick melee opponent that relies on speed and mobility rather than endurance.

Heavy Bandit:
A sturdier and more protected sword-wielding enemy with a heavier silhouette and a more armored design. This unit is suited to the role of a tougher melee adversary, capable of withstanding more damage while maintaining steady close-range pressure.

#### Main Villain and Final Boss

Velarayo:
Velarayo is the main villain and final boss of the game. As the wizard responsible for opening a portal to another realm, he is the central cause of the corruption that has spread throughout the kingdom. As the source of the conflict, he stands as the knight’s ultimate opponent and the final challenge of the game.
